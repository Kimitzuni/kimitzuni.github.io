Introduction

Hi. I'm Rebecca, and I'm a software developer from England, and have been using desktop Linux for over 10 years. I have experience with a good chunk of programming languages, with my personal favourite language being C. I'm currently a student at St Helens College, on the BTEC IT Level 2 course, and providing technical support for Linux users at the college, while such a niche thing to do - it is still a thing that I do. Alongside that I am also a software collector, both games and just general software, and a hardware collector. Currently I own 3 BBC Microcomputer Model B's, a Sinclair ZX Spectrum, a ThinkPad X230, T460S, T60 and have given an IBM ThinkPad 701C to IBM London. Whether or not they still have it is a different question.
